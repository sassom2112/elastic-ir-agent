## Recommended Actions
1. **Immediate Containment**: Isolate `MSEDGEWIN10`, `LAPTOP-JU4M3I0E`, and `DESKTOP-TTEQ6PR` from the network.
2. **Eradication**:
    - Investigate and remove `MalSeclogon.exe`, `PPLdump.exe`, `memViewData.hta`, `memViewData.jpg`, `KDECO.bat`, `SpoolFool.exe`, `EfsPotato.exe`, and `i.exe` from all affected hosts.
    - Review and remove any persistence mechanisms (e.g., scheduled tasks, registry modifications, services) created by the attacker.
    - Force password resets for all compromised users, especially `IEUser`, `bouss`, and `win10`.
3. **Recovery**: Restore affected systems from known good backups.
4. **Hardening**: Implement stronger endpoint detection and response (EDR) rules to detect and prevent the execution of known credential dumping tools and suspicious script interpreters. Review and update security awareness training for users regarding phishing and suspicious attachments.
